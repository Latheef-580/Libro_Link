How to Buy on LibroLink

A simple step-by-step guide to help you purchase your next great read with confidence.

  1. Browse Books: Use the search or browse categories to find books you love.
  2. Add to Cart: Click to add books to your cart.
  3. Review Cart: Click the cart icon to review your selected books.
  4. Checkout: Proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping and payment details.
  5. Order Confirmation: Receive an order summary and confirmation email.
  6. Track & Receive: Track your order and enjoy your new books!
