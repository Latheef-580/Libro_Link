Seller Guide

Everything you need to know to start selling books on LibroLink.

  1. Create an Account: Register as a seller or upgrade your account.
  2. List Your Books: Add book details, set prices, and upload clear images.
  3. Manage Orders: Track sales, respond to buyer queries, and update order status.
  4. Ship Promptly: Package books securely and ship within 2 business days.
  5. Get Paid: Receive payments directly to your bank account after order completion.
  6. Grow Your Reputation: Provide great service and earn positive reviews!

Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality images and detailed descriptions.
  • Respond to buyer messages promptly.
  • Offer competitive pricing and promotions.
  • Keep track of inventory to avoid overselling.
  • Stay updated with LibroLink policies and guidelines.
  • Participate in seller webinars and community forums.
