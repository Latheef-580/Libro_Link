Fees & Pricing

Understand our transparent fee structure for sellers and buyers.

  • Listing Fee: Free – No charge to list your books.
  • Commission: 10% of the final sale price (deducted after a successful sale).
  • Payment Processing: Included in commission, no extra charge.
  • Payouts: Free direct bank transfers for sellers (minimum payout: ₹100).
  • Shipping: Paid by buyer at checkout (varies by location/order value).

Example Pricing

For a book sold at ₹500:

  • Sale Price: ₹500
  • Commission (10%): ₹50
  • Total Earnings: ₹450 (after commission)
